To The Space:

In this trip, we go up to a specific altitude in space and then return back to the earth. It's a short trip that can be completed within a few hours. This is the most affordable space travel option, costing approximately $100,000.(including the ground-based training fee).

To The ISS:

In this journey, we visit the ISS (international space station), which is a modular space station in low earth orbit. The trip will take 15 (earth) days to complete, including  the 12 hour travel time. The cost of this trip will be $500,000. (including the ground-based training fee).

Fly Now !!

 

To The Moon:

During this trip, we will visit the moon, which is 384,400 km away from the earth. The trip will last 30 (earth) days, which is equal to one complete moon day. This will also include the travel time of 6 days. Our company has developed such a technology and established such an infrastructure that has allowed life to exist on the moon. The cost of this trip will be $1,000,000  (including the ground-based training fees).
