Using SPace For Good

We are one of the first commercial spaceline and vertically integrated aerospace company. We are developing and operating a new generation of space vehicles to open space for everyone. Our mission is to be the Spaceline for Earth, means we focus on using space for good while delivering an unparalleled customer experience. We recognize that improving access to space is a fundamentally challenging ambition. We will achieve it only in a spirit of collaboration and with a recognition that we must continually learn and can always improve.

Reusabilty

AstroScape believes a fully and rapidly reusable rocket is the pivotal breakthrough needed to substantially reduce the cost of space access.
While most rockets are designed to burn up on reentry, AstroScape rockets can not only withstand reentry but can also successfully land back on Earth and refly again.
